DOCX Converter

We currently support the following Microsoft Word converter: PDF to DOCX, ODT to DOCX, DOCM to DOCX, RTF to DOCX, WPD to DOCX, TXT to DOCX and many more. You can even convert images or ebooks to a DOCX document.

How to convert to DOCX?

  1. Upload the file you want to turn into DOCX.
  2. Extract text from a scanned PDF or image by clicking on the "Use OCR" option and choosing a source language (optional).
  3. Start the conversion process by clicking on "Start conversion".
